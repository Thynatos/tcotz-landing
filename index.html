Optimizing BobaCo's Future

A Production Planning Decision Support System for NESCO's Fastest-Growing Brand

↓

The Challenge

BobaCo, Turkey's first industrial-scale bubble tea manufacturer, faced critical production planning challenges during high-demand summer seasons.

🏭

Limited Capacity

Two production lines with strict constraints — each line can only produce one product type per day.

📈

Seasonal Demand

Summer peaks that outpace real-time production capacity, leading to excessive overtime and backlogs.

📋

Manual Planning

Semi-manual planning based on market intuition, with no mathematical optimization backing decisions.

⏰

Overtime Crisis

Averaging 28+ overtime days per year to recover from demand surges and delayed orders.

Our Solution

Hierarchical Decision Support System

We built a three-layer mathematical optimization framework that generates feasible production plans while considering demand uncertainty.

  • Two-Stage Stochastic Long-Term Model — Monthly aggregate planning with demand scenarios
  • MILP Short-Term Scheduler — Daily production decisions with machine-level assignments
  • Rolling Horizon Execution — Weekly replanning as new demand information arrives

Powered by Python, HiGHS solver, and delivered through an intuitive Streamlit interface.

Live Application

Deployed at NESCO through a 4-week pilot study. The system generates weekly production plans and raw material orders every Monday.
